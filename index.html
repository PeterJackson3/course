Dive into Web3 Realem : Web3 Fundamentals

What is the next evolution of the internet? What will drive the new online innovation? In this course, Web3 Fundamentals, you’ll be introduced to the technological pillars of Web3. First, you’ll explore the basics of blockchain. Next, you’ll discover how Ethereum is providing the fabric for a new kind of Web and with new kinds of applications. Finally, you’ll learn about the language and tools needed to build these new applications. When you’re finished with this course, you’ll have a firm foundation of the Web3 Fundamentals, and the knowledge needed to navigate the Web3 revolution!
